# Getting Started with Track Leader

Welcome to Track Leader! This guide will help you get started with creating segments, uploading activities, and competing on leaderboards.

## Creating an Account

1. Visit the Track Leader homepage
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign up"
3. Enter your name, email, and password
4. Click "Create account"

You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your activities page.

## Setting Up Your Profile

After creating your account, you can customize your profile:

1. Click on your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown
3. Add optional information:
   - **Display Name**: How you appear on leaderboards
   - **Age Group**: Enables age-based leaderboard filtering
   - **Gender**: Enables gender-based leaderboard filtering

Your profile information helps you compete against similar athletes in filtered leaderboards.

## Uploading Your First Activity

Track Leader accepts GPX files from any GPS device or fitness app:

1. Navigate to "Activities" in the main menu
2. Click "Upload Activity"
3. Select a GPX file from your computer
4. Add a title and description (optional)
5. Choose the activity type (Run, Ride, etc.)
6. Click "Upload"

Once uploaded, Track Leader automatically matches your activity against all existing segments.

## Exploring Segments

Segments are sections of trail where athletes compete for the best time:

1. Go to "Segments" in the main menu
2. Browse the list or switch to map view
3. Use filters to find segments by:
   - Activity type
   - Location
   - Distance

Click on any segment to see:
- The leaderboard
- Segment details (distance, elevation)
- Your personal records

## Understanding Leaderboards

Each segment has a leaderboard showing all recorded efforts:

- **KOM** (King of the Mountain): Fastest male athlete
- **QOM** (Queen of the Mountain): Fastest female athlete
- **PR** (Personal Record): Your best time on this segment

Use the filter buttons to view:
- Overall rankings
- Age group rankings
- Gender-specific rankings
